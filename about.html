Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about Aaterra Library.

Is Aaterra Library free to use?

Yes! Aaterra Library is completely free for all users. You can browse our entire collection, save favorites, and discover new books without any subscription fees or hidden costs.

How do I save books to my favorites?

Simply click the heart icon on any book card or book details page. The book will be instantly added to your personal favorites collection, accessible from the "Favorites" link in the navigation menu.

Can I download books to read offline?

Currently, we offer online reading only. However, we're working on an offline mode feature that will allow you to download your favorite books for reading without internet connection. Stay tuned for updates!

How often is the collection updated?

Our collection is updated weekly with new titles across all genres. We carefully curate each addition to ensure quality and diversity. Follow us on social media to get notified about the latest arrivals.
